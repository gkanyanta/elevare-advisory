The Methodology

A Proven 5-Step Approach

01
Phase One

Discovery

We begin every engagement by deeply understanding your context, your challenges, and your strategic objectives. This is not a box-ticking exercise — it is the foundation on which all subsequent work rests. We engage your key stakeholders, review existing evidence, and map the institutional landscape before committing to any approach.

Discovery ensures that our advice is grounded in your reality, not in generic frameworks or assumptions imported from other contexts.

Typical Deliverables
  • Context mapping report
  • Stakeholder interviews
  • Document review
  • Problem statement
  • Engagement scope brief
02
Phase Two

Analysis

We apply data-driven analysis and evidence benchmarks to interpret what the Discovery phase has uncovered. This includes quantitative data analysis, qualitative sense-making, and benchmarking against comparable institutions or contexts within the Southern African region and globally where relevant.

Our analysis is always conducted with an eye toward action — we synthesise findings into patterns and insights that can inform concrete decisions.

Typical Deliverables
  • Evidence synthesis report
  • Data analysis outputs
  • Benchmark comparison
  • Gap analysis
  • Diagnostic brief
03
Phase Three

Strategy

We develop tailored, decision-ready strategic frameworks — not lengthy documents that sit on shelves. Our strategy products are built for use: they are clear, prioritised, and linked directly to implementation pathways. We co-create these with your leadership team to ensure genuine buy-in and ownership.

Every strategy we develop answers three questions: What needs to change? How do we make it happen? How will we know it's working?

Typical Deliverables
  • Strategic framework
  • Theory of change
  • Prioritised action plan
  • Implementation roadmap
  • Decision-ready brief
04
Phase Four

Implementation

We do not disappear after handing over a strategy document. The Elevare Advisory offers active implementation support — embedding with your team as needed to support execution, navigate obstacles, facilitate key decisions, and maintain momentum through the inherent complexity of institutional change.

This is where most advisory firms step back. We step forward.

Typical Deliverables
  • Implementation support retainer
  • Facilitation of key sessions
  • Progress reporting
  • Obstacle resolution
  • Stakeholder coordination
05
Phase Five

Monitoring

We design monitoring systems from the outset — not as an afterthought. During the Monitoring phase, we track agreed outcomes against defined metrics, identify what is working and what needs to be refined, and support your team in building the internal capacity to sustain this learning loop independently.

Our goal is for every institution we work with to emerge stronger, smarter, and more self-sufficient — not dependent on perpetual consulting support.

Typical Deliverables
  • M&E framework
  • Progress dashboards
  • Learning reviews
  • Outcome reports
  • Refinement recommendations
What Guides Us

Guiding Principles

Evidence Over Opinion

Every recommendation we make is grounded in data, research, and contextual analysis — never intuition alone.

Human-Centric Lens

Systems change only when people change. We keep human dynamics at the centre of every strategy we design.

Practical Impact

We measure our success by what actually changes on the ground — not by the quality of the documents we produce.
