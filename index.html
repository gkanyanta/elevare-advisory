About The Elevare Advisory

A Zambian Consultancy Built for Lasting Impact

"We don't just advise — we partner with leaders to build institutions that endure."

The Elevare Advisory is a specialised consultancy that helps institutions move from ambition to measurable impact. We work at the intersection of evidence, dialogue, and human capital — three areas where strategies most often stall in execution and institutional reform.

Headquartered in Lusaka, we partner with multilateral institutions, governments, and leading enterprises across Zambia and the wider Southern African region.
